Rock Paper Scissors Game

About

Welcome to the Rock Paper Scissors game! This classic hand game is a simple yet exciting way to pass the time and challenge your friends. The rules are straightforward: rock beats scissors, scissors beats paper, and paper beats rock. It's a game of chance and strategy, where predicting your opponent's next move is key to winning.

Our version of the game allows you to play against the computer. Each round, you'll select rock, paper, or scissors, and the computer will do the same. The winner of each round is determined by the game's traditional rules. Keep track of your score and see if you can outsmart the computer!

Enjoy the game, and may the best player win!
